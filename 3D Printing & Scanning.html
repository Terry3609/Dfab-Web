3D PRINTING & SCANNING

There are a set of instruction to follow in order to have it propoly printed.

We started by drawing on paper the design we want and slowly drawing it in Computer Aided Design like AutoCAD,Sketch-Up and Fusion 360. All our drawing must be drawn in 3D in order to be printed.

Here's an example of drawing it in Sketch-up.

After you are done with your 3D design ,you are to exported it into a 3D printing software.

Ultimarker Cura is a software that is compatible with 3D printer in order to be able to print.

After you have exported it ,there are settings to be made like size,support,infill,speed,amount of material and the time of the artifact to be printed.

Here's a look at Ultimarker Cura software.
